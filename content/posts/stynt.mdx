## About

  

Stynt is the largest and fastest-growing employer of dental professionals across the United States of America. The startup offers a novel, AI-powered, humane experience for health professionals, combining the benefits of both temp and permanent employment at their desired location.

  
  

#### Type of engagement: Agency / Freelance platform

  

![Sample Image](/images/600x300.png)

## Challenge

  

To attract experienced and veteran dental professionals and hygienists as Stynt expands in different states

  

![Sample Image](/images/600x300.png)

  

## Solution

  

Developed copy for unique ads, videos, social media accounts, career pages, and other growth channels to attract and recruit the desired professionals

  

![Sample Image](/images/600x300.png)

## Effects

  

✨ Increased new sign-ups across the state

✨ Ongoing client relationship and social media content development for the brand

✨ Developed copy for product/service launches, e.g., Stynt Professional Coaching Program
